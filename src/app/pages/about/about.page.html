About

Qaza Tracker

This app helps you track your pending (qaza) prayers and provides intuitive UI for logging & updating your Salah balance.

Features

  • Manage all your balance prayers
  • Track your progress using charts
  • Export records as JSON/CSV data.
  • Stores everything your credentials in your phone, offline!
  • Simple notepad to save other necessary stuff
  • CRUD for all prayers with lovely UI
  • Offline storage, Open-source and hence trustworthy

User Guide

  1. Setup the master data in the beginning.
  2. Everytime you need to update the records, simply change the values and save.
  3. Daily logs can be added for your memory and motivation.
  4. In the "Track" section, see your progress instantly. As soon as you update the records, the changes will reflect.
  5. Notepad can be used to jot down & save other necessary stuff.
